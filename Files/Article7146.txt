<TITLE>KNOWLEDGE DATA SYSTEMS <KDSI> NAMES CHAIRMAN</TITLE>

former chairman, Jon Jacoby, vice chairman.

of <Mediflex Systems Corp>, and Jacoby is also its chief
financial officer.
