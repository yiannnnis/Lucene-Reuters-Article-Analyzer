<TITLE>HONEYWELL <HON> UNIT GETS COMPUTER CONTRACTS</TITLE>
<BODY>Honeywell Bull Inc, the newly formed
company jointly owned by Honeywell Inc, <NEC Corp> of Japan and
<Compagnie des Machines Bull> of France, received four
contracts for mainframe computers and related equipment.
    Honeywell Bull said it received separate contracts from
Coleco Industries <CLO>; <North West Securities>, a
British-based financial institution; the DeKalb Country School
System of Georgia, and Banca Popolare De Vicenza, an Italian
regional bank.
