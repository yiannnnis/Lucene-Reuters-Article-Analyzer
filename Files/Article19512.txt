<TITLE>BEST PRODUCTS <BES> TO BUY TWO MLN OF ITS SHARES</TITLE>
<BODY>Best Products Co Inc said it plans
to repurchase up to two mln shares of its common stock for
employee benefit plans and general corporate purposes.
    A company spokesman said no further details are available
on the repurchase plan. He said the company now has about 27
mln shares outstanding.
