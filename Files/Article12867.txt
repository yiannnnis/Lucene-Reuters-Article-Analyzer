<TITLE>AMERICAN PRECISION <APR>, WORKERS HAVE AGREEMENT</TITLE>
<BODY>American Precision Industries Inc
said members of United Auto Workers Local 1416 and the
company's Delevan and Deltran divisions reached agreement on a
three-year contract.
    The company provided no details other than the contract is
effective from March 29, 1987, through March 31, 1990.
