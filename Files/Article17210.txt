<TITLE>MUNSON TRANSPORTATION DELAYS INITIAL OFFER</TITLE>
<BODY>Munson Transportation Inc said it
postponed its proposed initial public offering due to weakness
in the price of truck stocks.
    Munson is a trucking company that transports food products
and other general commodities, primarily in the Midwestern,
Central and Northeastern sections of the U.S.
