<TITLE>BABCOCK UNIT WINS ORDER FOR VENEZUELAN POWER PLANT</TITLE>
<BODY>Deutsche Babcock AG
<DBCG.F> said its wholly-owned Borsig GmbH subsidiary has won a
78 mln mark order to build a conventional power station block
for Venezuela's state-owned electricity company CADAFE.
    It said the installation, which can be powered by oil or
gas, should go on stream at the end of 1988.
