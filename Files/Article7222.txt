<TITLE>CHAMPION <CHA> IN 285 MLN DLR EXPANSION</TITLE>
<BODY>Champion International Corp said
its board approved a 285 mln dlrs expansion of its mill in
Hinton, Alberta, Canada.
    The project will double capacity of the mill's pulping
operation to 424,000 short tonnes a year.
    Champion expects the expansion to be completed by 1989.
