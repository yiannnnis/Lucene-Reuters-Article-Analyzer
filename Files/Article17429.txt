<TITLE>MIM RAISES COPPER PRICES BY 20 DOLLARS</TITLE>
<BODY>Mount Isa Mines Ltd <MIMA.S> said it
raised its copper prices by 20 dlrs to 2,220 dlrs per tonne for
cathodes and to 2,387.50 dlrs per tonne for standard rods,
effective June 17.
