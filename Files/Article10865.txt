<TITLE>PHILADELPHIA EXCHANGE SETS FAR EAST MARKETING</TITLE>
<BODY>The <Philadelphia Stock Exchange>
said it has established a Far East marketing division based in
Hong Kong to try to broaden participation among international
banks, multinational corporations and trading firms in its
foreign currency options market.
    The exchange said the new division will shortly open a
representative office in Tokyo.
