<TITLE>LM ERICSSON <ERICY> UNIT INTRODUCING PRODUCT</TITLE>
<BODY><LM Ericsson Telephone Co> of
Sweden said its Ericsson unit in the U.S. introduced a cellular
system for small cellular telephone markets.
    The company said its CMS 8800/S system incorporates
Ericsson hardware and software.
    It said extra base stations and voice channels can be added
to the system in modular blocks.
