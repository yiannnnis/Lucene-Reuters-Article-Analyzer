<TITLE>MERCANTILE STORES <MST> SETS 2-1/2-FOR-1 SPLIT</TITLE>
<BODY>Mercantile Stores Co Inc said
its stockholders approved a 2-1/2-for-1 common stock split.
    Mercantile said the record date for the split was June one
and that it expects to mail the additional shares resulting
from the split on June 15.
