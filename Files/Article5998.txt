<TITLE>SPAIN TO SUSPEND LEMON EXPORTS FROM THURSDAY</TITLE>
<BODY>Spain's citrus management
committee said it was suspending lemon exports from Thursday in
view of the likelihood of new European Community tariffs.
    A committee spokesman said the body would meet next week to
review the situation and decide whether to lift the suspension.
