<TITLE>BANCTEC <BTEC> GETS LARGEST ORDER EVER</TITLE>
<BODY>BancTec Inc said it received an order
for image processing equipment valued at six mln dlrs, its
largest order ever.
    BancTec said the order, expected to be shipped during the
current fiscal year, was placed by a major international
company, which it did not name.
