<TITLE>MGM/UA <MGM> JOINS CHINA FILM CORP PACT</TITLE>
<BODY>MGM/UA Communications Co said
it has joined Paramount Pictures and MCA Inc's <MCA> Universal
Studios in their agreement with China Film Corp of Beijing to
distribute motion pictures in China.
    MGM said it is also joining a venture to license two hours
a week of primetime television programming to China's national
television network.
    Revenues will be derived from advertising time for U.S. and
foreign companies, it said.
